Juniper Lopez Troxel

I graduated from the Connecting Point School of Massage and Spa Therapies in Telluride, Colorado in 1996. Shortly thereafter, I moved to Jackson Hole and began practicing massage therapy at Southern Touch Massage and at spas and hotels including Amangani, Spring Creek Ranch, and The Alpine House. In 1999 I started Three Peaks Massage and moved into my current downtown office. I am nationally certified and a member of the American Massage Therapy Association. My style emphasizes sports stretching and deep tissue massage for the active recreationalist and I also offer hot stone therapy, pre-natal and reflexology treatments. Each session reflects thought, attention to detail and the utmost professionalism. I feel lucky enough to have established a long standing clientele of repeat costumers for 18 years and counting. I enjoy spending my free time hiking and skiing and savoring all Jackson Hole has to offer.
